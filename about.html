About Me

Hi, my online pseudonym is Shung3, but you can call me Shung. I created Tycables to hopefully provide a better experience to those who are interested in buying or making their own high quality cables.

On my homepage, you should find links to configurators to commission a cable from me, or to buy the loose parts to put together yourself.

On our top navigation bar, you may also look through my blog, where I go over the basic tools required to build a cable. In the future, I plan on creating videos or visual step-by-step walkthroughs to help quell user confusion and aid those who require more visual instructions to learn

With my website, I hope to provide more people the knowledge on how to start making cables and make it more accessible to everyone.

If anybody is interested, the site name has no real deep meaning, I just thought it sounded nice.
